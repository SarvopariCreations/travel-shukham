Turkiye Highlights with Hot Springs Visit & Bosphorus Cruise

8.0 Very good 1 review

Ancient cities, towering Turkish minarets and mesmerising natural landscapes await on this unforgettable small-group tour, uncovering layers of Turkiye’s history and culture. See where sultans used to live at the decorated Topkapi Palace, and marvel at the grandeur of the Hagia Sophia in Istanbul. Float between two continents on an unforgettable cruise upon the Bosphorus Strait. Tunnel through towns of ‘fairy chimneys’ in Cappadocia – and optionally seize the opportunity to soar above the stone formations on a breathtaking hot air balloon ride as the sun rises (at an additional cost). Journey to Pamukkale, where terraces of white mineral-rich waters cascade down the hillside and discover the ancient city of Ephesus, a testament to the marvels of Roman engineering.

Travel comfortably with spacious private coach transport, airport transfers and internal flights across Turkiye. From the grandeur of Istanbul, to the surreal landscapes of Cappadocia, the ancient wonders of Ephesus and the coastal allure of Izmir, take yourself on the journey of a lifetime steeped in history, enjoying local dining and unique experiences along the way.

Location

Paradise Koh Yao

Paradise Koh Yao is located on the dreamy island of Koh Yao Noi, an untouched paradise easily reachable with a scenic speedboat ride from Phuket through the famous limestone karsts of Phang Nga Bay. The resort is located a 15-minute drive and a 50-minute speed boat transfer from Phuket Airport (HKT). Nestled within national reserve tropical rainforest, in its own secluded bay overlooking Phang Nga, this five-star retreat is famous for snorkelling, kayaking, hiking and island hopping.

Scheduled return airport transfers from Phuket International Airport with speedboat connection from Yacht Haven Marina are included for 7+ night stays only, see Fine Print for details.
